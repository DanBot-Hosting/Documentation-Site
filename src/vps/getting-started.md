# Getting Started - VPS

VPS Accounts are managed through our billing site for purchasing, and the operation controls done through VPS Site.

---

## Sites:

- Billing: https://billing.danbot.host
- VPS: https://vps.danbot.cloud

---

## Software:

- We utilize [Paymenter Billing](https://paymenter.org/) software to manage your VPS billing and tickets.
- We utilize the [VirtFusion](https://virtfusion.com/) software to virtually manage all VPS services that we provide.

## Getting Started:

- First, create an account on https://billing.danbot.host.
